FAQ

Is this an official Star Wars production?

No. This is a non-commercial student project and is not affiliated with Lucasfilm or Disney.

How are donations used?

Donations support production costs only, including wardrobe, props, locations, logistics, stunt safety, and post-production.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes. You can be listed as “Anonymous” or not listed publicly at all.

Are tier benefits based on the total amount donated?

Yes. Recognition is based on the total amount received from a supporter.

Who do I contact for larger gifts or special support?

Email Starwarsatucsd@gmail.com and we’ll coordinate.
