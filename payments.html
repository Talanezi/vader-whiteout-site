FAQ

Is this an official Star Wars production?

No. This is a non-commercial student project and is not affiliated with Lucasfilm or Disney.

How are donations used?

Donations support production costs only: wardrobe, props, locations/logistics, stunts safety, and post-production (color and sound).

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes. If you prefer, we can list you as “Anonymous” or not list your name at all.

Who do I contact for larger gifts or in-kind support?

Email Starwarsatucsd@gmail.com and we’ll coordinate.
