Story

    In the shadowed realm between life and death, Darth Vader faces the echoes of his past and the haunting truth of what his power has cost him.
    Visual Language

    Lookbook

    Palette

    Black-and-white with rare hints of cold blue — the world feels bleached and quiet, with blue only where the veil thins.

    Framing

    Wide, contemplative shots place Vader against vast horizons, emphasizing solitude and scale.

    Optics

    Dreamlike distortion through wide primes and low contrast; subtle flares and reflections suggest reality folding in on itself.

    FAQ

    • Is this official? No. This is a non-commercial student project, not affiliated with Lucasfilm/Disney.
    • How are funds handled? Through the Star Wars Club at UC San Diego and the Vader: Whiteout Production team for production costs only.
    • Safety? Choreography and set safety are core. No live weapons. Trained supervision.

    About the Club. The Star Wars Club at UC San Diego is a student organization producing non-commercial fan projects and community events. Follow updates on Instagram at @starwarsatucsd.

    Safety Policy

    We prioritize cast and crew safety: rehearsed choreography, no live weapons, site permissions, and trained on-set supervision.
