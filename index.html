Story

    In the shadowed realm between life and death, Darth Vader faces the echoes of his past and the haunting truth of what his power has cost him.
    Visual Language

    Lookbook

    Palette

    Ethereal and surreal, subtle purples weave through natural light. Hints of crimson and amber pulse around Vader, adding warmth to an otherwise otherworldly calm.

    Framing

    Wide, contemplative shots place Vader against vast horizons, emphasizing solitude and scale.

    Optics

    Dreamlike distortion through wide primes and low contrast; flares, reflections, and depth shifts give the sense of a reality folding in on itself.
